how it works

Short lessons.
Real recognition.

1
Take the quiz
12 scenario questions. No right answers. You get a personal constellation — a map of how you naturally connect, and where the mirror has the most to show you.
2
Follow your path
Based on your constellation, the app recommends where to look first. Lessons are short, grounded in real scenarios — texts, conversations, moments you've lived.
3
Meet your mirror
The app watches how you respond. Over time it reflects back what it notices — specific, warm, and only when you're ready to see it.
